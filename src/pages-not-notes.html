Make pages, not notes

  • If it's interesting, we share it.
  • If it's based on research, we refer to it.
  • By writing pages, it forces to formalize one's thinking

Asset management

Before writing a page, reflect on the classification of the information you're sharing:
  • Public: Available to anyone
  • Internal: Available only to people inside the organisation
  • Confidential: Avaialble only to people related to the project
  • Restricted: Need to know basis only
If the info is anything else but public, consider adding security features such as login or encryption.
